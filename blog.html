STARLINK

March 21, 2024

Starlink is a satellite internet constellation developed by SpaceX, founded by Elon Musk. The project aims to provide high-speed, low-latency internet connectivity globally, especially in underserved and remote areas. Starlink consists of thousands of small satellites in low Earth orbit, offering speeds of up to 1 Gbps and latency as low as 20 ms. The service promises to bridge the digital divide, support IoT applications, and enable seamless communication worldwide. With its innovative technology and ambitious goals, Starlink is revolutionizing the future of internet connectivity.

ENVISION GLASSES

February 14, 2024

Envision Glasses are AI-powered smartglasses with an integrated camera and built-in speakers that speak out the visual world. The core technology that powers Eye in Ai is artificial intelligence and computer vision. The glasses use artificial intelligence algorithms to interpret the user's surroundings and convert visual information into sound, allowing for improved navigation and understanding of the environment for blind individuals.

All Envision Glasses features: Instant Text, Scan Text, Batch Scan, Call an Ally, Call Aira, Describe Scene, Detect Light, Recognise Cash, Detect Colors, Find People, Find Objects, Teach a Face and Explore.
